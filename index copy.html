Welcome

About

Hi, I'm Jeff Zeiss. I am first and foremost a full stack web programmer in addition to being a musician, artist, and woodworker. Having spent the vast majority of my adult life creating art, working with my hands and exploring and climbing all over the west coast, I settled down to focus on and hone my art and music. I have been based out of the front range of CO since the summer of 2012. Over the last decade I explored digital art and music production, doing 2d, 3d and motion projects. I found myself doing small bits of programming in basic HTML. Towards the end of the decade I found my self becoming more and more enthralled with web programming. This came with the discovery that programming though strict in formal execution is actually mind bogglingly expansive in its potential for creativity.
I've been hooked ever since this fundamental paradigm shift and have sought to know all I can about the field. I was priviledged to get accepted into a bootcamp with Thinkful. I studied in their Engineering Immersion course and the rest is History!
It is my goal to create as many web applications as I can in this short life! "Make hay while sun shines" as the saying goes. I'm always seeking like minded and innovative folks with whom I can help dreams come true and push the boundaries of what's possible with coding!
Let's make something together!
